Shayari for You 💌✨

Tere khayalon mein hi khoya rehta hoon, 🥰
Tujhse juda ho kar bhi tera hi rehta hoon.
Mohabbat hai tu meri, saza nahi, 💖
Tujhse door jaane ka khayal bhi sahna nahi. 💔

Mohabbat ka ehsaas tu hai, 💞
Mere har khwab ka aas tu hai.
Dil karta hai tujhe har pal dekhta rahoon. 😍
Mere har din ki shuruaat tu hai. 🌅
